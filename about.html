About Sterling.

A Portrait of the Artist as a Young Man

My name is Sterling Hanak, and I am both a software developer and a writer. I graduated from Kent State University, where my creativity and passion for writing led me to obtain a Bachelor's degree in English. Over the years I also developed a highly adaptable work ethic by excelling at several different jobs as diverse as retail, a cook, investigative research, and even management. Most recently, a desire to combine my passion for writing with my enthusiasm for technology caused me to enroll at Tech Elevator; a 14-week coding bootcamp based in Cleveland Ohio. The program allowed me to fully embrace my technical interests by learning to program primarily in the C# programming language. I was able to add to my skill set so that I would be talented at writing not only fiction, but clean and efficient code as well. I'll never wish to stop learning, however, and I look forward to pursuing a career where I will be able to dive even deeper into software development and acquire the ability to program in additional languages.
